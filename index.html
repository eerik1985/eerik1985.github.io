What we like

You know, we really envy the way children read. They never get bored of their favourite story. They keep the characters into their imagination and take them by the hand to new adventures. They walk and talk and become good friends. We really love the way children pass fictional bariers like a drop of rasberry juice through a white paper towel.

Who we are

We’re a team of people that enjoy reading and playing. We love art and technology as well. We may appear adults, but don’t get fooled by the display. Our moms can guarantee we’re far from growing up. But we think that we have a lot to offer and what other way can we do that if not through digital books. So `forget` your tablet around the house and let your kids discover great books on it. Make time to read to them. Or why not enjoy it yourself, it’ll be our little secret.
